Survey: Enhancing Your Sports Betting Data Experience

      • Social media sentiment/buzz
      • Other (Please specify: ____)
  • Q3: On a scale of 1 to 5, how overwhelming do you find the amount of data available on typical sports betting platforms?

  • Q4: How much time do you typically spend gathering all the information you need before placing a bet?

      • Less than 15 minutes
      • 15-30 minutes
      • 30-60 minutes

  • Intro: "We're exploring ways to make sports betting data more comprehensive and easier to understand."

  • Q1: Imagine a platform that automatically shows you key contextual information (like weather, player injuries, recent team news, and social media buzz) alongside the betting odds for a game. How valuable would this be to you?

    • Likert Scale (1 = Not valuable, 5 = Extremely valuable)

  • Q2: How often do you find yourself switching between different websites or apps to gather all the relevant information for a single bet?

    • Multiple Choice:
      • Always
      • Often
      • Sometimes
      • Rarely
      • Never

  • Q3: If you had a tool that presented all relevant contextual data for a game in one place, how much more confident would you feel in making your betting decisions?

    • Likert Scale (1 = No more confident, 5 = Much more confident)

  • Q4: What specific types of contextual information (beyond basic stats and odds) do you feel are most crucial for making informed betting decisions, but are often hard to find or connect?

    • Text Box: (Open-ended user input)

3. Potential Solutions & Preferences

  • Q1: Would you be interested in a feature that provides a "snapshot" or summary of critical contextual factors for an upcoming game?
    • Multiple Choice:
      • Yes, definitely
      • Maybe
      • No, not really
  • Q2: How important is it for this contextual information to be updated in real-time or near real-time?
    • Likert Scale (1 = Not important, 5 = Extremely important)
  • Q3: What would be the biggest benefit of having all contextual information readily available on one platform?
    • Text Box: (Open-ended user input)
